The Best Caesar Salad

Prep: 20 mins Total: 25 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, halved
  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) olive oil
  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, torn into pieces
  • 1 cup (250 ml) croutons
  • Additional Parmesan shavings for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a wooden salad bowl, rub the inside with the garlic halves.
  2. Add anchovies and mash them into a paste with a fork.
  3. Whisk in egg yolk, lemon juice, and mustard until smooth.
  4. Gradually whisk in olive oil until dressing emulsifies.
  5. Stir in grated Parmesan and season with pepper.
  6. Add lettuce and toss gently to coat with dressing.
  7. Add croutons and toss again.
  8. Garnish with Parmesan shavings and serve immediately.

Chef's Notes

For a more intense flavor, let the dressing sit for 30 minutes before using. The egg yolk will cook slightly from the acid in the lemon juice, but for complete food safety, use pasteurized eggs.
